---
title: Issuing a clearance
description: How to issue a departure clearance from a strip.
---

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

<Steps>

1. Click the **Destination / Stand** block on a strip to open the flight plan.

2. Validate that the route, SID, SSR, EOBT, and runway are correct.

   ![Flight plan editor](../../../assets/ekch-clr-del-fpl.jpg)

3. Click **CLD** to issue the clearance and move the strip to **Cleared**.

</Steps>
